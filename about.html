About CrazyCattle3D

CrazyCattle3D began as a passion project by a team of developers who grew up on farms and wanted to share the authentic farming experience with the world. What started as a small indie game has grown into one of the most realistic farming simulators available online.

Our team spent years researching cattle farming practices, working with real farmers, and developing cutting-edge 3D graphics to create a game that's both educational and entertaining.

CrazyCattle3D is constantly evolving, with regular updates adding new features, animals, and gameplay mechanics based on player feedback and agricultural developments.

Game Details

  • Genre: Farming Simulation
  • Platform: Browser-based
  • Initial Release: 2023
  • Developer: CrazyCattle Studios
  • Age Rating: Suitable for all ages
  • Languages: English, Spanish, French, German, Chinese

Our Mission

We strive to create the most authentic digital farming experience while making it accessible to everyone. Our goal is to entertain players while also educating them about sustainable farming practices and animal husbandry.
